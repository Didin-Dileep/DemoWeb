About Us

Built on compassion, action, and accountability.

NayePankh Foundation is a community-focused non-profit dedicated to creating practical, lasting change. We partner with local leaders, donors, and volunteers to support people who need access to education, healthcare awareness, livelihood opportunities, and safe spaces.

What drives us

We believe social impact should be transparent, inclusive, and measurable. Every initiative is designed to be relevant to the communities we serve and easy for volunteers to join.

Mission & Vision

Clear purpose today, stronger communities tomorrow.

Mission

To empower underserved communities through education, compassionate support, and volunteer-led programs that improve daily life and build confidence for the future.

Vision

A society where opportunity is not limited by background, and where every person can access the support needed to learn, grow, and thrive with dignity.

Programs & Activities

Meaningful programs designed for real-world impact.

Education Support

School kits, tutoring drives, scholarship assistance, and learning support for children and youth.

Health Awareness

Wellness camps, hygiene awareness, first-aid sessions, and community health education programs.

Women Empowerment

Skill-building workshops, self-help initiatives, and livelihood training to promote independence.

Community Relief

Distribution of essential supplies, food drives, and support during urgent community needs.

Youth Volunteering

Volunteer campaigns that help young people contribute time, skills, and energy toward service.

Awareness Campaigns

Events focused on social issues, environment, and responsible civic participation.
